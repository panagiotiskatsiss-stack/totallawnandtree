Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about land clearing

How much does land clearing cost? +
Land clearing costs vary based on lot size, vegetation density, terrain, and debris disposal needs. Light clearing might cost $1,000-$2,000 per acre, while heavy clearing with large trees can run $3,000-$6,000+ per acre. We provide free on-site estimates for accurate pricing.
Do I need permits for land clearing? +
Permit requirements vary by location and project scope. Many areas require permits for clearing above a certain acreage or for removing specific tree species. We can help you understand local requirements and assist with the permit process if needed.
How long does land clearing take? +
Timeline depends on project size and complexity. A typical residential lot can often be cleared in 1-3 days. Larger or more complex projects may take longer. We'll provide a time estimate during your consultation.
What happens to the debris? +
We offer several options: forestry mulching converts vegetation to beneficial mulch left on-site, or we can haul away all debris for disposal. If desired, we can also leave cut wood for firewood. We'll discuss options during your estimate.

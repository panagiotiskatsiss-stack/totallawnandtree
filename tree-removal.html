When Do You Need Tree Removal?

Not every tree problem requires removal, but there are situations when it's the best‚Äîor only‚Äîoption:

  • Dead or Dying Trees: Trees that are dead, diseased beyond treatment, or in severe decline pose falling risks and should be removed promptly.
  • Storm Damage: Trees with major structural damage from storms, including split trunks or large broken limbs, may be unsafe to save.
  • Hazardous Location: Trees growing too close to power lines, buildings, or other structures may need removal to prevent damage.
  • Root Problems: Trees with roots damaging foundations, driveways, or underground utilities.
  • Construction or Landscaping: Trees that need to be cleared for building projects or landscape redesigns.
  • Overcrowding: Removing select trees to improve the health and appearance of remaining trees.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our tree removal services

How much does tree removal cost in {{CITY}}? +
Tree removal costs vary based on size, location, and complexity. Small trees typically cost $200-$500, medium trees $500-$1,000, and large trees $1,000-$2,000+. Factors like proximity to structures, power lines, and accessibility affect pricing. Contact us for a free, accurate estimate for your specific tree.
Do I need a permit to remove a tree? +
Permit requirements vary by municipality in {{STATE}}. Some areas require permits for trees over a certain diameter or for specific species. We can help you determine if a permit is needed and assist with the application process.
How long does tree removal take? +
Most residential tree removals take 2-5 hours depending on size and complexity. Large or hazardous trees may require a full day. Very large or complex removals might span multiple days. We'll provide a time estimate during your free consultation.
What happens to the wood after removal? +
We offer several options: We can haul away all debris, chip branches into mulch for your garden, or cut the trunk into firewood lengths if you'd like to keep it. Just let us know your preference when scheduling.
Do you remove the stump too? +
Stump grinding is available as an additional service. Many customers choose to have the stump ground to below ground level so they can plant grass or a new tree in its place. We'll include stump grinding pricing in your estimate if you're interested.
